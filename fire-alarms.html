FAQ

Common Questions

How often should fire alarms be tested?

Weekly testing is recommended, with professional servicing at least twice a year for full compliance.

Conventional vs addressable fire alarm?

Conventional systems divide property into zones. Addressable systems pinpoint the exact detector, preferred for larger buildings.

Do fire alarms need to connect to other security systems?

Integration with CCTV and access control enhances safety — doors can auto-unlock during a fire for safe evacuation.

Are customised systems more expensive?

They may involve higher initial investment but are designed for your exact requirements, avoiding unnecessary features.

What maintenance is required after installation?

Routine checks of detectors, batteries, control panels, and sounders. Professional servicing identifies issues early.
