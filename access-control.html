YOUR QUESTIONS

Frequently Asked

What's the difference between access control and traditional locks?

Traditional locks rely on physical keys, which can be lost or copied. Access control uses digital credentials, making them more secure and easier to manage.

Can systems be customised for different properties?

Yes, door entry systems can be tailored to suit both domestic and commercial needs.

How do access control systems improve convenience?

Employees can use a single card for multiple areas. Homeowners can grant temporary codes to visitors.

Are they suitable for businesses with high staff turnover?

Absolutely. Access rights can be updated or revoked immediately, ensuring only current employees have entry.
